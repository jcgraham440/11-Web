Max Temperature

vs. Max Temperature

The temperature plot above shows that temperatures, as expected, max out at the equator. What is slightly surprising is that the curve doesn't show the same drop-off in the southern hemisphere as one moves away from the equator. This might be due to the fact that the southern hemisphere contains more ocean than the northern. Alternatively, it might be due to the fact that in January, it is summer time in the southern hemisphere. To get a better sense of the temperature/latitude correlation, readings should be made on the equinoxes.
